Our Story

Decatur's Favorite BBQ Joint

Soul Pig BBQ has been bringing Texas-style slow-smoked barbecue to downtown Decatur since day one. Co-owners Mark Graves and Chad Silva have built a reputation for brisket so tender it practically melts, pulled pork cooked low and slow over applewood, and craft beer that pairs perfectly with everything on the menu.

With daily specials that keep regulars coming back, house-made BBQ sauces you can't get anywhere else, and an atmosphere that feels like a neighborhood hangout — Soul Pig is the real deal.
